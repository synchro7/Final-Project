About This Game
Konami will once again showcase their premier Football title – Pro Evolution Soccer 2015 boosted by the coveted Fox Engine!

With bigger and broader team selection, long time fans of ProEvo will be pleased to know that almost every aspect of this game has been improved – it is a true evolution of a long time series, especially on next gen consoles.

Presenting an action-packed gaming experience with complex game-plays and distinct player models beyond imaginations, ProEvo 2015 is a sight to behold. Hyper realistic player models in PES 2015 harness the distinct and compelling on-ball and off-ball skills corresponding to real life actions of the soccer stadium.

Feel the intensity of electrifying moment in the soccer stadium of PES 2015, providing advance gameplay, unique kicking techniques and high-level Artificial Intelligence. PES 2015 strengthens each individual player's arsenal to harness an exclusive sets of skills; deliver goal-winning kicks to overwhelm opponents and forge a legend in the PES history.
