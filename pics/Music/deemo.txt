Product Detail
---------
Deemo is a rhythm game developed by Rayark Games
Deemo uses both instrumental and vocal music to accompany its rhythm game elements. The selection consists of works from composers from multiple regions including Taiwan, Japan, South Korea, Hong Kong. Notable contributors include Earthbound Papas and guitarist Shinichi Kobayashi. Deemo also uses songs that was in Cytus, another rhythm game by the same developer.
